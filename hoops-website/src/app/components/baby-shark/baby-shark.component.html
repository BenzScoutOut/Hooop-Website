HOOOP is a Pinkfong Baby Shark’s official authorized licensing agent in Thailand. Pinkfong is a global entertainment brand that creates award-winning kids’ content for families around the world. The brand’s flagship IP “Pinkfong Baby Shark” became a breakout sensation when it was launched on Pinkfong’s YouTube channel in November 2015. “Pinkfong Baby Shark” took the world by storm amassing 88 billion views as of June 2021 becoming the No.1 most viewed YouTube video, while reaching the world’s first RIAA certified diamond status as a children’s single for surpassing 10 million unit sales in the US.
